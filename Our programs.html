Programs offered at Medical Laboratory Training School Jinja

Being a specialized institution, there are two programs offered;

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology

Minimum Entry Requirements

There are two categories of candidates enrolled on this program;

1. Senior six leavers: The minimum entry requirement for senior six leavers is a Principle pass in Biology and two subsidiary passes in the other two specified science subjects.

2. Upgrading students from a certificate in Medical laboratory techniques. One must have been registered by the responsible professional body and should have at least served for two years. Or as per the current curriculum, a candidate may apply soon after qualifying with a certificate in medical laboratory techniques. 

Duration of the Program: 

Diploma takes three years 

Programme Intake 

July/August

Certificate in Medical Laboratory Technology

Minimum Entry Requirements

Candidates enrolled on this program, go through interviews organized by Health Education and Training department of the Ministry of Education and Sports. Candidates are from senior four and must have passed mathematics, English, Biology, Chemistry and Physics. 

Duration of the Program: 

Certificate takes 2 Years of Study

ABOUT MLTSJ

Medical Laboratory Training School Jinja (MLTS) is a government-aided institution and registered by the National Council for Higher Education.
 
It was established in 1967 as training site for laboratory attendants for JinjaHospital
