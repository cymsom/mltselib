MLTSJ Library & Resource Center

The Medical Lab Technology Training Library and Resource Centre serves all the entire school and both programes. users including staff, students and other registered library users.


Library Objectives: 

To make the Library as easy to use independently as possible and manage the collections in ways that assist users in finding the information they need;
To make access and use of e-Library services as easy as possible;
To maintain a silent cool study environment;
To place help and support at the centre of the Library services;
To raise awareness of Library services available to students and all staff

ABOUT MLTSJ

Medical Laboratory Training School Jinja (MLTS) is a government-aided institution and registered by the National Council for Higher Education.
 
It was established in 1967 as training site for laboratory attendants for JinjaHospital
